Frequently Asked Question(FAQs)

Where do I find my enrolled courses?

Navigating your eguru Account is super easy just follow the below steps:
Login to your eguru Account.
Click on "My Courses" at top of the page.
Click on "Courses" Tab under "My Courses".
And there, you would see your active course plan. For more information, click on "View Details" for the course.

How can I pay ?

You can pay for eguru using a credit/debit card, net-banking, and using online wallet.

Can we choose the timing of the eguru mock tests?

You can give it anytime.

I need some counseling on my performance in the Tests/Assignments. Whom should I contact?

You need to drop an email with the course details and registered email id to admin@eguru.com. Once you drop the email the concerned team will connect with you shortly.

Where can I get solutions for previous year's question papers?
  1. Go to eguru's website and click on "Resourses" given on the top of the webpage.
  2. Click on "Previous Year Papers" in the menu.
  3. Select the grade you want solutions for.
